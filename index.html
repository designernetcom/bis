Our Vision

Developing unique individuals with a vision beyond horizon, thought beyond conception and action beyond perception to lead and guide the generations on the never ending path of progress.

Our Mission

Creating a caring and collaborative learning community, to ensure that each student achieves intellectual and personal excellence and is well prepred for future careers and pathways.

BSI Philosophy

Bloomingdale believes that the teaching is not about knowledge transfer, but fostering excellence, evolving cognitive skills.

The education system in Bloomingdale is a blended form of traditional & modern teaching of Teaching Learning Process.

The education system of the school besides providing an all-round education intellectually, socially emotionally and physically, offers guidance in crucial thinking, social openness and purposeful research. Students are given opportunities to develop their own talents as well as to be aware of national and global issues while contributing to the healthy development of needs in the community through activities like Smart Service Learning, Home Economics, Work Experience.

The complete education system is based on true Indian values with all modern techniques and technologies for overall personality development of the students.

Each child is unique and is a treasure house of talent. Helping children Explore, Experience & Excel is the sole aim of our education system.

The School provides exposure training and platform through various Cubs to showcase their talents. Whether it is dance, music theatre, subject wise clubs or sports, children are given ample opportunities to identity and develop their abilities and areas of interest. The goal is not to help each child make a career out of his/her talent but to get in touch with his/her inner creativity.
